XBIKE

Established in 1989, XBike Bikes has a vision to become a global quality bicycle brand and ready to compete in the international market. Starting as a small company oriented to produce bicycles specifically for export, the first 10 years were a learning process and XBike's focus was to prepare for independent brand development. Starting in 2000, XBike began to expand its factory and invest in high-tech equipment of international standards. XBike has a self-contained manufacturing, assembly and distribution network that meets world standards so it can continue to control every aspect from initial idea to final delivery of high quality bicycles. Having a vision to become a global brand that continues to prioritize authenticity, originality and quality, until now XBike Bikes has been distributed in 500 outlets spread across the world, and distributed to 33 countries.
